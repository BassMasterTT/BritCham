Why Join?

We open the door to connecting your business with all that is British in trade ethos, education & integrity.

Join in our fun, social opportunities and inspiring networking events and benefit your business through a valuable business connection you may not even realise is out there.

Be part of The Greater Bay Area British Business Directory.

Enjoy British social connections in Macau & Hong Kong, through our links with British Chambers throughout the world and especially with the Macau European Chamber of Commerce.

BritCham Macao offers a range of membership categories:

  • Sovereign - High Tier Membership
  • Noble - High Tier Membership
  • Sterling - High Tier Membership
  • Corporate
  • Small to Medium Enterprise (SME)
  • Individual

More than Pop Culture! – BritCham Macao extols fundamental principles and harmony in good business.
