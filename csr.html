Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR)



Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) Being only a small Business Chamber, Britcham Macao really must cut its clothes according to its cloth. Firstly we must recognise our need to raise monies to ensure our offices are run in the efficient, pro-active manner required of our Chamber.


We began fundraising by running our Gala Balls in 2009. It was from 2009’s Ball profits that we created our Chamber’s CSR Fund. Happily, this makes the time spent organising events so much more gratifying for the Ball Committee. Today we commit a minimum of 50% of Ball profits to our CSR Fund.
