Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR)


Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) Being only a small Business Chamber, Britcham Macao really must cut its clothes according to its cloth. Firstly we must recognise our need to raise monies to ensure our offices are run in the efficient, pro-active manner required of our Chamber.


We began fundraising by running our Gala Balls in 2009. It was from 2009’s Ball profits that we created our Chamber’s CSR Fund. Happily, this makes the time spent organising events so much more gratifying for the Ball Committee. Today we commit a minimum of 50% of Ball profits to our CSR Fund. Since 2009, our Chamber has distributed a total of Mop 548,685, as follows:


Scholarship fees for one girl studying at UMAC:

Mop60,200

Scholarship fees for a second girl studying at UMAC:

Mop60,300

Donation to one School of The Nations’ Pupil in need:

Mop30,000

Donations to the Association of Rehabilitation of Drug Abusers of Macau (ARTM), totalling:

Mop77,685

School Fees for one boy who needed to continue his education at Pui Ching School:

Mop30,000

Additional financial support for one Fountain of Hope Child to follow his dream of obtaining a Business Management degree at Plymouth University in UK (graduating in summer 2018):

Mop60,000

Support to the Scholarship Fund of The School of The Nations, enabling one orphaned scholar to reach University in Hong Kong in mid- 2018:

Mop100,000

Fellowship Programme at The University of St Joseph:

Mop80,000

Assist a gifted Macao Student to attend a summer internship at Oxford University, UK:

Mop50,000

These may appear seemingly small actions, but they are making big impacts to the lives of individuals and their families in our beloved Macao.


We thank our Members and friends for supporting our Chamber’s Annual Ball. We are particularly grateful to our Ball Sponsors for enabling us to achieve this position of charitable giving.
