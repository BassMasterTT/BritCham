About the Chamber

Welcome to The British Chamber of Commerce in Macao

Britcham Macao was launched in July 2006 as The British Business Association of Macao (BBAM) to promote and represent British business interests in the region.

Relaunched as Britcham Macao in 2019, we further our Members' network connections and raise awareness of their companies through high quality events: linking them with all that is British in trade ethos, education, integrity and standards.

Companies and individuals do not have to be British to join our Chamber.

All our members enjoy celebrating British business codes of practice & values through fun, social opportunities and inspiring breakfast & luncheon gatherings. Britcham Macao is your link to other British Chambers.
